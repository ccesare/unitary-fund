Celebrating quantum open-source software contributors: Announcing the 2020 Wittek prize winner with QOSF



1 February, 2021
Unitary Fund Team

Over the past six months, we have collaborated with the Quantum Open Source Foundation (QOSF) to design, roll-out and assign the 2020 Wittek quantum prize for an individual making, with their work, exceptional contributions to the quantum open-source software ecosystem. We are glad to share the announcement of the 2020 Wittek quantum prize winner. Many thanks to the Unitary Fund Advisory Board, which, together with the Tech Team, has been volunteering time to review nominations of an exceptional level. This prize acknowledges the importance of the impact that an individual can have in the ecosystem, for the benefit of the whole quantum science community.
